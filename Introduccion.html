Introducción

En la actualidad acceder a servicios de medicina es un desafío significativo para las poblaciones que viven en zonas de difícil acceso, las condiciones de infraestructura, limitaciones geográficas, edad avanzada de los pacientes, entre otras circunstancias complican significativamente la entrega de suministros médicos.

Frente a esta problemática este proyecto busca de manera innovadora fabricar un dron que permita beneficiar y solucionar la entrega de medicamentos con un peso aproximado de 150g.

Abstract

We, a group of Industrial Automation students, aim to develop a flying drone throughout our college years to deliver medical resources. Our acquired knowledge will be the soul of this project and the driving force behind our motivation.

Currently, accessing medical services is a significant challenge for populations in remote areas. Infrastructure conditions, geographical limitations, and the advanced age of patients, among other factors, greatly hinder the delivery of medical supplies.

Rationale

The design and manufacturing of a drone specialized in delivering medications to remote areas not only represents a technical challenge but also an opportunity to positively transform health, employment, and innovation in these communities.

By overcoming these challenges, a solid foundation is established to ensure equitable and efficient access to essential healthcare services, thereby promoting sustainable development and significantly improving the quality of life for those who need it most.
